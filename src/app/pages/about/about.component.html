About Us

A Sanctuary Where Time Slows Down

EXPERIENCE UDAIPUR’S CULTURE THROUGH CRAFT, CUISINE, AND COMMUNITY

Nestled amidst rolling hills and verdant landscapes, Shivrati Vilas is more than just a retreat—it's an invitation to reconnect with nature's rhythm. Our boutique farmstay blends rustic authenticity with thoughtful modern comforts.

Shivarti Vilas Himmat Bagh is a truly remarkable heritage property, steeped in the legacy of the noble Mewar dynasty. This historic farmhouse has been lovingly restored and reimagined as a one-of-a-kind farmstay, where heritage charm seamlessly meets contemporary comfort. Its distinctive colonial architecture sets it apart from typical homestays in and around Udaipur, offering guests an experience that is both elegant and timeless.
